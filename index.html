privacy

0bsidian stores nothing about you on our servers.

Tasks, timers, chat history, and buddy choice live only in your browser's localStorage. Clearing site data wipes everything.

Chat requests go to Cloudflare Workers AI over HTTPS and are not logged by us. Ephemeral KV entries (rate-limit buckets keyed by IP, a 90-second "studying now" heartbeat, and a 30-minute Turnstile verification flag) expire on their own. No PII, no analytics, no trackers, no cookies beyond what Turnstile requires for bot prevention.

Source code is public on GitHub — audit it yourself.

# what's new

## april 2026

**break tagging** — when you end a focus session and enter a break, a modal lets you tag it: #meals, #bathroom, #rest, #other. tags are editable and optional.

**subject-based sessions** — clicking play now asks what you're studying. sessions are logged under the subject name so your history is actually meaningful.

**chat threads** — up to 10 conversation threads saved in the sidebar. auto-titled from your first message. switch between them without losing context.

**smart presets** — add a subject containing "UWorld" and the focus timer auto-sets to 60 minutes. "First Aid" or "Bootcamp" sets it to 25 minutes.

**resume button** — the play button now correctly shows "resume" when a session is paused mid-way.

**what's new tab** — you're looking at it.

---

## march 2026

**focus mode** — count-up timer that tracks working and break phases separately. no countdown pressure.

**session log** — every completed focus block is logged with duration and timestamp. rename or delete entries inline.

**study companion** — the little character top-left reacts to your study state and occasionally checks in.

**pomodoro mode** — classic 25/5 countdown with cycle tracking and tomato indicators.

## february 2026

**tasks panel** — add subjects with colour labels, nest tasks under each subject, check them off.

**dark theme** — pitch-black background, warm terracotta accent, Fraunces serif throughout.

**cloudflare ai chat** — powered by llama 3.1 on the edge. no logs, no tracking.

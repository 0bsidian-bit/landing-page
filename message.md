# Hello from Lokesh

I'm a junior resident physician currently in postgraduate training, spending most of my days between wards, textbooks, and a homelab that never quite behaves itself.

0bsidian started as a way to stay focused during long study sessions — a pomodoro timer I could actually trust, a task list that didn't need an account, and an AI I could ask questions without switching contexts. It grew from there.

## Why I built this

Most productivity apps are bloated, require sign-ups, and send your data somewhere you'd rather it not go. This one doesn't. Everything lives in your browser. The source code is on GitHub — read it, fork it, use it.

## A note on the AI

The chat is powered by a small, fast Llama model running on Cloudflare's edge. It's not GPT-4. It's not trying to be. It's a focused assistant that works well for medical and general questions — and doesn't require a subscription.
